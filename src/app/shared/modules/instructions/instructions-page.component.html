1127 ELEVEN TWENTY SEVEN CONSTRUCTION, INC,

NCMS has been requested to audit your company’s Pipeline Safety drug and alcohol program for the following client(s):

  • ARB Midstream, LLC
  • Dominion Energy East Ohio
  • Dominion Energy Services
  • NiSource Corporate Services

These clients have expectations for a compliant Pipeline Safety Program in order to obtain and maintain a satisfactory status.

Who We Are

NCMS assists our clients with meeting their expectations for a safe, reliable, and environmentally-sound operation of their facilities. NCMS along with our clients have developed a process to help ensure Contractor compliance with regards to a Pipeline Safety Drug and Alcohol Program.

Your company will have employees performing job functions that are considered “covered positions” under DOT/PHMSA (Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration). Our clients require a review of your Pipeline Safety drug/alcohol program to ensure it will meet their expectations.
